About

I am a software engineer and educator with over 14 years of experience building large-scale distributed systems, currently a Lead Software Engineer at JPMorgan Chase and formerly an Assistant Professor teaching graduate courses on enterprise architecture, distributed systems, and databases. I build and measure systems, with a bias toward empirical, build-and-measure inquiry over speculation.
